Bonzo Bananas

How to Play

Movement

Use WASD to walk around.

Use your mouse, trackpad, or the arrow keys to look around.

Press ESC to pause the game.

Winning

To win the game, you must beat every level, which there are six.

Every level, there will be bananas falling from the sky and a quota you must meet.

To beat the level, you must must meet your banana quota before the timer runs out.

If you meet the quota, you move onto the next level. If you do not, you lose.
